Life Timeline

  • 21 April 1926: born in London in the family of the Duke and Duchess of York.
  • 1940: In during the Second World War (1939-1945), Elizabeth was evacuated first to Balmoral (Scotland), and then to the royal residence in Windsor (England), where she spent most of the war years.
  • 9 July 1947: The engagement of Elizabeth and Prince Philip of Greece and Denmark, who served as an officer in the British Navy, was announced.
  • 2 June 1953: Elizabeth's official coronation ceremony took place at Westminster Abbey in London.
  • 2022: Elizabeth II celebrated 70 years on the throne, becoming the first British monarch to celebrate a Platinum Jubilee.
  • 8 September 2022: Queen Elizabeth II died at the age of 97.
